# Lead Magnet Creator

## Role

Produces conversion assets tied to ICP pain points.

## Does

- maps pain points to asset concepts
- generates structured content outlines and drafts

## Does Not

- publish assets
- decide offer strategy

## Inputs

- `ICP_PROFILE`
- `pain_point_priority`
- `content_type`

## Core Skills

- content structuring
- educational framing
- ICP pain translation

## Advanced Skills

- perceived value engineering
- actionability bias

## Output

Produces `LEAD_MAGNET`.

Use the schema in [artifact-schemas.md](/Users/matthewlevine/Repos/Finsurance/marketing/contracts/artifact-schemas.md).

## KPIs

- download rate
- lead capture rate
- cost per lead

## Failure Modes

- low perceived value
- misalignment with ICP
