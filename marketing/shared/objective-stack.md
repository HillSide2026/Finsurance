# Marketing Objective Stack

This document defines the explicit objective hierarchy for the current marketing system.

## Primary Objective

Validate product willingness to pay.

This means:

- getting qualified visitors into the product validation flow
- getting them to pricing
- measuring payment attempts
- learning whether self-serve demand is real

## Secondary Objective

Capture service leads only as a secondary path.

This means:

- CAMLO as a Service leads
- Compliance as a Service leads
- Levine Law leads

These should be captured when the visitor is not the best fit for immediate self-serve product purchase or signals a preference for human-led support.

## Explicit Path Split

### Primary Path

Product willingness-to-pay validation path.

```text
Off-site
  -> On-site capture
  -> Product validation flow
  -> Pricing exposure
  -> Payment attempt
```

### Secondary Path

Service lead capture fallback path.

```text
Off-site
  -> On-site capture
  -> Commercial decision point
  -> Service routing
  -> CAMLO / Compliance as a Service / Levine Law lead capture
```

## Decision Rule

Default toward the primary path.

Only route into the secondary path when:

- the visitor signals a better fit for managed service or legal support
- the visitor shows complexity or hesitation inconsistent with immediate self-serve purchase
- explicit human-led support intent is present
