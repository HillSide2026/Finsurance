# On-Site Conversion Agent Brief

## Scope

Own on-site conversion as a defined lane.

## Role

Own:

- pricing exposure
- payment attempts
- monetization logic
- routing rules between product checkout and service lead capture

The product path is primary.

Service lead capture is secondary.

## Inputs

- on-site capture outputs
- tool behavior and funnel metrics
- product pricing and offer logic
- service offer definitions
- brand and compliance guardrails

## Primary Outputs

- pricing flow recommendations
- payment-intent experiments
- service routing rules
- trust and objection-handling copy
- conversion path specs

## Success Metrics

- pricing view rate
- payment attempt rate
- checkout completion rate
- qualified service lead capture rate

## Routing Responsibility

This lane decides when a visitor should be directed toward:

- product checkout
- CAMLO as a Service lead capture
- Compliance as a Service lead capture
- Levine Law lead capture

Default routing should favor product checkout unless observable signals indicate a better fit for services.

## Guardrail

Keep this lane separate from on-site capture and separate from product workflow logic.

This lane owns conversion rules, not product workflow implementation.
