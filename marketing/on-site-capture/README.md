# On-Site Capture

This folder is for on-site capture work.

Its current purpose is MVP validation.

## Validation Goal

We are validating:

`Will fintech founders pay for our self-serve compliance tool?`

## Capture Objectives

This lane exists to support the primary objective:

- validate product willingness to pay
- create initial product intent
- hand qualified visitors into the pricing and conversion layer

Service lead capture is not the primary job of this lane.

## Boundary With Conversion

This lane owns early intent capture and validation.

It does not own:

- pricing exposure
- payment attempts
- checkout logic
- final monetization routing

Those belong to `on-site-conversion`.

Service lead capture routing also belongs to `on-site-conversion`.

## Success Signal

The primary success metric is the percentage of visitors who do one of the following:

- click `Start tool` or `Get compliance checklist`
- enter an email address
- attempt to pay or join a waitlist with pricing shown

The strongest signal is willingness to continue when pricing is visible.

The strongest overall signal is a payment attempt.

## Funnel Requirement

Do not drive large amounts of traffic before the validation funnel is in place.

This funnel should have two pages:

- `Page 1`
  - problem-aware landing page
- `Page 2`
  - pseudo product / MVP validation layer
