# Off-Site Agent Brief

## Scope

Own off-site awareness and traffic generation.

## Current Channel

LinkedIn is the current hypothesis.

The best off-site source is not yet known.

## Current Objective

Drive qualified traffic from tested off-site channels into the on-site capture funnel.

## Experiment System Responsibility

This lane must operate through explicit channel tests.

Every channel test should define:

- hypothesis
- destination
- budget limit
- timebox
- success thresholds
- kill criteria

## Deliverables

- audience hypotheses
- channel experiment plans
- channel experiment reports
- post concepts
- content calendar drafts
- founder-led messaging tests
- campaign learnings

## Guardrail

Do not redesign on-site capture or product conversion flows from this lane.

Hand traffic and messaging assumptions into the on-site capture lane.

Do not treat LinkedIn as a validated channel until performance data supports it.

Do not scale any off-site source unless it passes the experiment framework.
