# LinkedIn Hypothesis 01

## Channel

LinkedIn

## Hypothesis ID

`LINKEDIN_H01`

## Hypothesis

LinkedIn can generate qualified fintech founder traffic that reaches the on-site funnel and produces meaningful commercial signals.

## Destination

Send traffic into the on-site capture funnel, then measure commercial outcomes through the on-site conversion layer.

## Test Sequence

### Stage 1

Organic micro-test.

Structure:

- publish `6` founder-led posts over `14` days
- use one clear CTA into the on-site funnel
- log traffic and downstream behavior

Budget limit:

- `CAD 50` maximum paid spend

### Stage 2

Paid pilot, only if Stage 1 passes.

Structure:

- run a focused `14` day paid or boosted test
- reuse the best-performing message angle from Stage 1

Budget limit:

- `CAD 300` maximum paid spend

## Stage 1 Success Thresholds

- qualified sessions from LinkedIn `>= 25`
- primary CTA click-through from LinkedIn sessions `>= 10%`
- one of the following commercial outcomes:
  - pricing views `>= 5`
  - payment attempts `>= 1`
  - qualified service leads `>= 2`

## Stage 1 Kill Criteria

- fewer than `10` qualified sessions after the full timebox
- budget cap reached with `0` payment attempts and `0` qualified service leads
- primary CTA click-through from LinkedIn sessions `< 5%`

## Stage 2 Success Thresholds

- qualified sessions from LinkedIn `>= 50`
- cost per qualified session `<= CAD 25`
- pricing or service-route reach rate `>= 10%`
- one of the following commercial outcomes:
  - payment attempts `>= 2`
  - qualified service leads `>= 4`

## Stage 2 Kill Criteria

- budget cap reached without a strong commercial signal
- cost per qualified session exceeds `CAD 25`
- pricing or service-route reach rate remains `< 5%`

## Decision Rule

- `pass`
  - LinkedIn advances to the next stage or careful scale testing
- `iterate`
  - keep LinkedIn alive, but change message angle, audience, or destination
- `kill`
  - stop spending on LinkedIn and test the next source hypothesis

## Notes

These are v1 thresholds for early validation.

They should be updated as real data accumulates.
